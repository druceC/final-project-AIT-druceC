Kung Pao Chicken

  • Ingredients:
    • Chicken breast
    • Peanuts
    • Bell peppers
    • Onions
    • Soy sauce
    • Rice vinegar
    • Chili paste
  • Instructions: Stir-fry chicken, peanuts, and vegetables in a wok. Add a sauce made with soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili paste.
  • Nutrition Info:
    • Proteins: 20g
    • Carbs: 15g
    • Fats: 8g
    • Calories: 250
